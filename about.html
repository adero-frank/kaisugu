About Kaisugu Tea

We make excellent Tea Products.

Our History

Established in 1928 amidst the lush highlands west of the Rift Valley in Kericho, Kaisugu Limited has evolved into a beacon of excellence in the tea industry.

Nestled at an altitude of 2300 meters above sea level, approximately 275 km from Nairobi, our journey began as a humble tea estate rooted in a commitment to quality and community.

About Us

The success history of Kaisugu Limited in 97 years

Our tea is one of the most popular drinks in the world

Kaisugu Limited is a well-known tea brand in Kenya that produces internationally-certified organic tea.This brand claim to be the only 100% organic tea estate. Our Plantations

Our plantation initiative focuses on growing diverse and sustainable crops that contribute to the environment and local communities. We aim to enhance biodiversity, provide green spaces, and ensure the longevity of our ecosystems.

Core Values - Kaisugu Tea

As we reflect on our journey, we envision a future steeped in continued excellence. Join us as we cultivate the finest teas, nurture communities, and craft a legacy that stands the test of time. Indulge in the essence of Kaisugu - where every sip tells a story, and every leaf carries a legacy.

Vision

To be a leading global source of excellent tea, building a legacy of sustainability, creativity, and unrivaled quality that enhances the tea-drinking experience for tea lovers throughout the world.

Mission

We aspire to produce great teas in a socially and economically responsible manner, focusing on sustainability, fair trade, and community empowerment to benefit both tea lovers and local communities.

Core Values

  • Quality: Commitment to obtaining and creating the finest tea leaves via careful processes to assure consistent high quality.
  • Community Empowerment: Promoting fair trade practices, investing in their development, and ensuring their well-being in order to support and empower local communities.
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty, openness, and ethical business practices in all facets of the organization’s operations.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Meeting the demands of customers by providing great products and experiences that exceed expectations.
  • Education and Awareness: Promoting tea culture by teaching customers on the origins, benefits, and kinds of tea, as well as advocating responsible usage.
